Mediocre Ending: Turning Back

You take a deep breath and decide that whatever awaits inside the house is not worth the risk. You turn back the way you came, quickening your pace.

The wind howls behind you as if it’s trying to pull you back, but you don’t stop. Soon, you’re back at the entrance of the yard, your heartbeat finally slowing.

You escape the house unscathed, but a gnawing sense of regret lingers. What could you have uncovered if you had dared to enter?

You’ve escaped the house’s dangers... but the mystery remains unsolved.
