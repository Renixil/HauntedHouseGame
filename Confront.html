Good Ending: The Spirit is Freed

Summoning your courage, you step forward. The shadowy figure becomes clearer—it is the lost child from the diary. His hollow eyes lock onto yours.

Suddenly, the house shakes as you feel a presence leave. The child fades away, and the oppressive atmosphere lifts. You’ve freed the lost spirit.

The house falls into peaceful silence, and you walk away, knowing you’ve put a lost soul to rest.

You made the right choices.
