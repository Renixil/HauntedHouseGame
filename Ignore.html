Ignoring the Sound

You decide to brush off the strange giggling, telling yourself it’s just the wind playing tricks. You continue walking around the house, but the unsettling feeling gnaws at you.

The farther you go, the more the shadows around you seem to shift and stretch. The trees sway unnaturally, and you feel eyes on you, watching your every step.

Ahead, the back door of the house looms in the distance. It’s slightly ajar, creaking with the wind. Do you dare to approach it, or turn back now?

The house waits patiently for your next move...
