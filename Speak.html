Good Ending: Peace Restored

You calmly speak to the shadowy figure, asking it why it’s haunting this house. The figure hesitates, then speaks in a soft voice. It tells you the tragic story of its past, of a family torn apart by grief.

Your understanding and compassion help the spirit find peace. Slowly, the shadow dissipates, and the house becomes silent.

You’ve freed the lost soul, bringing peace to this haunted place.

You have saved the day.
